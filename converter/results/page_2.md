## 3.2. Program Requirements (Electives b ): (12 credit hours)

Students are required to take 12 credits as elective courses. In addition to the courses listed in the following table:

- Students may select 12 credits of 300 level and above from required/elective courses of Cybersecurity or Data Science tracks that are not listed in the table below to fulfill the elective requirements.
- Students may also select a maximum of six credits of 300 level and above courses from other departments in the School of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology to fulfill the elective requirements.
